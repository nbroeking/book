Mockup Questions

Who are we?

We are Revolver!

What do we do?

We deliver your food to you any day or night!

Who can use Revolver?

Anybody! As long as you have a computer and internet connection you can get food delivered anywhere?

Who can provide food?

Anyone! Revolver supports any restaurant, bakery, or other buisness to sign up and start selling food. Drivers are also welcome to sign in and start driving!

Attriubutes

We Track Name, Stars, Location, Type of Food, and the Delivery Time!!

Where are we?

Boulder CO! But we will be expanding to other places soon! Stay tuned!

Are we expensive?

Not at all! We charge the price of the food from the restaurant + a $5 doller delivery fee!

Looking for a job?

We pay $3.00 per delivery. Sign up now and start making your fortune!
